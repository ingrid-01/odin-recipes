Recipes

By Suzanne Douglass


Description

You know when you want to try a new recipe so you have a peak inside your fridge and cupboard. But to your complete surprise, you don't have the needed ingredients?

Babe, me too.

But this Suzie from '50s couldn't care less. She still went for it, and when it tasted bad, she blamed her friend.

Let's learn from her and try her admirable antics.

Ingredients

  • Some potatoes, if not, rice
  • Some paprika, if not, other spice
  • A half can of tomato sauce, if not, a whole can of tomato paste

Steps

  1. Bring a pan to medium heat, and follow ya girl Suzie.
  2. I didn't have potatoes so I substituted rice.
  3. I didn't have paprika so I used another spice.
  4. I didn't have tomato sauce; I used tomato paste,
  5. A whole can, not a half can; I don't believe in waste.
  6. Check your result, and if bad, blame the person who gave you the recipe! (That's me!)
  7. A friend gave me the recipe. She said you couldn't beat it.
  8. There must be something wrong with her. I couldn't even eat it.
