Recipe for a Soap Box Derby

By Mary Lee Hahn, 2015


Description

I have never been to a Soap Box Derby. I've just seen it in a movie or two.

So I have no idea what this person is talking about, but I just thought it was neat.

Shoutout to all the poets around the world!!!

Ingredients

  • the Great Depression
  • some memories
  • some snow
  • some hills
  • some sleds
  • some thrills
  • a group of camaraderie
  • 2 cups of intuitive engineering
  • a box of scraps of wood and salvaged wheels
  • a few long hot lazy summer days
  • a chunk of gravity

Steps

  1. Begin by simmering the Great Depression until you have a thick broth of thrift and innovation.
  2. Season with memories -- snow, hill, sled, thrill -- just enough to whet an appetite for speed.
  3. Gather camaraderie, intuitive engineering, scraps of wood and salvaged wheels, and long hot lazy summer days.
  4. Combine ingredients and serve with a heaping side dish fo gravity. Finish with wild cheering and trophies.
