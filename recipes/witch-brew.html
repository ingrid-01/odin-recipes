Description

In the classic Shakespeare tragedy, Macbeth, the three witches gather an unusual group of ingredients to make a potent mixture.

This brew eggs Macbeth on to encourage his ambition, which ultimately leads to his demise

Who's up for a shot?

Ingredients

  • A fillet of a fenny snake
  • An eye of a newt (or some mustard seeds)
  • A toe of a frog (or some buttercup flowers)
  • A wool of bat (or some holly leaves)
  • A tongue of a dog (or a gympsyflower from the Genus Hound's Tounge)
  • An adder's fork (or a bunch of Least Adder's tongue)
  • A blind-worm's sting
  • A Lizard's leg
  • An owlet's wing
